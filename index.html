Casual gaming

The goal of OHN is to provide a place for fighting game enthusiasts to gather and share their passion. As such, casual setups will be available throughout the weekend for all games in the official tournament line-up. DIY setups will also be available for casual play outside of tournament hours.

Only attendees that have registered as competitors are permitted to play casual games in the OHN venue.

Rules

All official tournaments at OHN are run in accordance with the official OzHadou tournament rules. All competitors at OHN are expected to familiarise themselves with these rules before the competition begins.

These rules can change at any time without prior notice. It is your responsibility to make sure you are up to date with the information that is displayed here.

All tournaments will be Double Elimination, played in 16 or 32 player pools (dependent on the total entrants). The top 2 players from each of the pools will go into the next round until there are only 8 players remaining. Those players will then compete in the top 8 brackets on the Sunday.

There are additional rules relating to the hardware being used at OHN, as well as rules specific to each official tournament game. All competitors must read the game specific rules for OHN prior to the event.
