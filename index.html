OHN14 is an official Ranking Event for
Capcom Pro Tour (CPT) Asia 2016

OHN14 is an official Capcom Pro Tour Asia 2016 Ranking Event

Players who place top 16 in Street Fighter V (SFV) at OHN14 will earn Tier 2 points towards their ranking in both the Asia and Global leaderboards, both of which can lead to spots in Capcom Cup.

The winner of SFV at OHN14 will qualify for the Asia Regional Finals premier event, where Tier 1 points for overall ranking can be earned. The winner of the Asia Regional Finals qualifies for Capcom Cup.

For players that have already received ranking points at other CPT Asia events such as Battle Arena Melbourne 8, OHN14 is a great opportunity for competitors seeking to win one of the highly coveted 32 qualification spots.

Please see the official CPT website for more information.

Casual gaming

Casual setups will be available throughout the weekend for all games in the official tournament line-up. DIY setups will also be available for casual play outside of tournament hours.

Only registered competitors are permitted to play casual games at OHN14.

Venue

OHN14 will be held at the Australia Ballroom at The Menzies Hotel, Sydney. The Menzies is located in the heart of central Sydney, moments away from Darling Harbour, Sydney Opera House, The Rocks and Circular Quay; and right across the street from Wynyard Station.

If you choose to book accomodation at the venue, call the Menzies directly and quote the discount code 160826. This allows you to book at a rate of $245 per night for a Standard Room.

Rules

All official tournaments at OHN are run in accordance with the official OzHadou tournament rules. In addition, there are rules specific to OHN14, covering each game in the official line-up and any particulars regarding the hardware for those games. All competitors are assumed to have read and understood these rules prior to registering and competing at OHN14.

These rules can change at any time without prior notice. It is your responsibility to make sure you are up to date with all rules information provided here.

All official tournaments will be Double Elimination, played in 16- or 32-player pools (depending on registration numbers). The top 2 players from each pool will advance to the next round until only 8 players remain. Those players will then compete in the top-8 brackets on Sunday.

Registration

All OHN14 attendees must register before 22 Aug.
On-the-day registrations are strictly not accepted.

To play games at OHN14, purchase a competitor pass. Casual play for all games is available to all competitors regardless of tournament participation; but competitors must also pay tournament entry fees for each game they wish to compete in.

If you're happy to just watch and not play, purchase a spectator pass.

Register before midnight on 31 July to receive a discount on your competitor pass. Early registration also gives you a chance to purchase the official OHN14 shirt. Shirts are only available via the registration process and can be collected when you arrive and sign in at OHN14.

* Passes are limited, so be sure to get in early to save money and secure your spot.
** No refunds will be issued if you cannot attend due to change of mind or personal circumstances.
